Frequently Asked Questions

VirtualDJ vs Serato DJ — which is better?

Serato DJ Pro is the industry standard for club and hip-hop DJs with the best scratch performance. VirtualDJ offers more features (video, karaoke, stems) and hardware compatibility at a lower price. Best DJ software depends on your use case.

Is VirtualDJ free?

VirtualDJ Home Edition is completely free for non-commercial use. Commercial use (paid gigs) requires a license: VirtualDJ Pro costs $299 one-time or $9.99/month.

Does VirtualDJ work with Pioneer controllers?

Yes. VirtualDJ has the widest hardware compatibility of any DJ software — supporting nearly every controller via built-in mappings. Pioneer DDJ, CDJ, and XDJ units are all supported.

Serato vs VirtualDJ for weddings and mobile DJs?

VirtualDJ is preferred for mobile DJs and weddings due to its karaoke, video, and extensive request management features. Serato is preferred for pure DJing without those extras.

Can I transfer my music library from Serato to VirtualDJ?

Yes. VirtualDJ can read your Serato library including cue points and BPM data. Import via File > Import from Library menu. Note that some cue points and loops may not transfer perfectly.

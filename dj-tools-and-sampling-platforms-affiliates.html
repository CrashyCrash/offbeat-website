  • Controller compatibility: Software that natively supports major plug-and-play controllers (Pioneer DDJ, Numark, Hercules, Native Instruments) saves time and setup frustration.
  • Recording & stems export: Practice recording and exporting mixes is useful for review and building a demo.
  • Library management & streaming: Good tagging, playlists, and integration with streaming services (where licensed) keep your music organized and legal for venues that allow streamed tracks.

  • Algoriddim djay: Superb for macOS/iPad users—very intuitive, strong streaming integrations, and touch-first workflows.

  • Free / Entry level (0–$50): Serato DJ Lite, VirtualDJ Home, and Algoriddim djay Free editions let you experiment without investment. Expect limits on audio outputs, effects, and streaming sources but full core mixing functions.

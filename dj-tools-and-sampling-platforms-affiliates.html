You must clearly disclose your affiliate relationship using prominent labels, such as "Paid Partnership" or "Commission Earned," at the beginning of your content rather than hiding it in a footer. Furthermore, new "AI Transparency Acts" require you to disclose if the review or promotional content itself was generated using AI. Failure to do so can lead to severe penalties from the FTC and other global regulators. Best practice is to include a clear "Ethics Statement" explaining your testing process and why you recommend the tool, ensuring that your recommendations are based on empirical evidence and actual usage.

FAQ

Is the DDJ FLX10 standalone? ▾
No, the DDJ-FLX10 requires a laptop running rekordbox or Serato DJ Pro.
Does it work with Serato and rekordbox? ▾
Yes — it ships with rekordbox and Serato DJ Pro licenses included.
What are the main features of the DDJ-FLX10? ▾
Stems separation, 8.1" jog wheels, 3-band channel EQ, Sound Color FX, and Beat FX.
Is it suitable for beginners? ▾
Not ideal for beginners — the DDJ-200 or DDJ-400 are better starting points.
What comes in the box? ▾
DDJ-FLX10, USB cable, power cable, and access cards for rekordbox and Serato DJ Pro.

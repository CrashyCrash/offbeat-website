Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Numark Scratch 2026 compatible with other DJ software?

Yes, while it is optimized for Serato DJ Lite and Pro, the Numark Scratch can also work with other DJ software that supports MIDI mapping, although the experience may vary.

Can I use the Numark Scratch 2026 for live performances?

Absolutely. The controller is designed for both practice and live gigs, providing the necessary features and connectivity to support performances.

What is the difference between Serato DJ Lite and Pro?

Serato DJ Lite is a free version with basic features, while Serato DJ Pro offers advanced capabilities such as more effects, audio routing options, and additional performance features.

How does the Numark Scratch compare to the Pioneer DDJ-FLX4?

The DDJ-FLX4 is more feature-rich with performance pads and advanced effects but lacks the dedicated scratching focus of the Numark Scratch, making it less suitable for turntablism.

Can I replace the crossfader on the Numark Scratch?

Yes, the Numark Scratch features a magnetic crossfader that is easily replaceable, which is a significant advantage for DJs who frequently use this feature.

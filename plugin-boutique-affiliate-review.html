Frequently Asked Questions

What commission does Plugin Boutique pay affiliates?

Plugin Boutique pays 25% commission on all plugin sales and 20% on hardware. Commissions are tracked for 30 days after a user clicks your link. There's no minimum sale threshold, and payments are made monthly via PayPal when your balance exceeds 50 GBP (~$63 USD).

How do I join the Plugin Boutique affiliate program?

Visit pluginboutique.com/affiliates and create a free account. Approval is typically instant for anyone with a website or YouTube channel. Once approved, you get access to a dashboard with deep links, banners, and a real-time earnings tracker. No minimum traffic requirement for initial approval.

What types of plugins sell best on Plugin Boutique?

Synthesizers (Serum, Vital), reverb and delay effects, and mastering tools consistently top Plugin Boutique's bestsellers. Seasonal sales (Black Friday, summer sales) spike conversions significantly - plan review content to publish 2-3 weeks before major sale events.

Is Plugin Boutique's affiliate program better than Native Instruments?

Plugin Boutique pays a higher percentage (25% vs NI's 10%) and covers a broader catalog including third-party plugins. NI's program has higher average order values (KOMPLETE bundles run $200-$600). For affiliates focused on a wide plugin library, Plugin Boutique wins on rate; NI wins on ticket size.

Does Plugin Boutique offer affiliate support?

Yes - Plugin Boutique provides an affiliate support team via email, regular newsletters with upcoming promotions, and a curated list of upcoming sales you can prepare content for. They also offer personalized affiliate links that bypass the discovery step for high-purchase-intent pages.

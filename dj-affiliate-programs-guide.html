DJ Affiliate Programs 2026: Earn Up to $15 Per Referral

In the world of DJing, affiliate marketing offers a unique opportunity for influencers, content creators, and music enthusiasts to monetize their passion. From music distribution to DJ software, affiliate programs are a lucrative way to earn commissions. In this article, we'll explore the affiliate programs offered by Splice, Serato, and DistroKid, two popular platforms in the DJing space, as well as Pioneer DJ, a market leader in DJ gear.

Splice Affiliate Program

Splice is a popular music production platform that offers a range of DJ software, including the Serato-compatible DJ Studio software. The Splice affiliate program is a great way for influencers to earn money on every referral. With a 15-day cookie duration, affiliates can earn a commission on new subscriptions and trial sign-ups. Commission rates vary from $10 to $15 per referral, depending on the plan. The application process takes 1-3 days to process, and accepted affiliates can use trackable links to promote the program. Splice is a great choice for affiliates looking to earn substantial commissions on music production and DJing software.

DistroKid Affiliate Program

DistroKid is another prominent player in the music distribution space, offering an innovative music distribution platform for DJs and musicians. The company has an affiliate program that offers commission rates on qualifying purchases, with an earning structure that varies depending on the plan. Influencers and content creators can apply to become DistroKid affiliates, with an optional influencer program offering different earning structures.

Pioneer DJ Affiliate Program (Absent for 2026)

Unfortunately, as of 2026, Pioneer DJ does not have an affiliate program. The company focuses on direct product sales, and no official announcements regarding an affiliate program have been made.

Serato Affiliate Program (via Splice)

Serato, a popular DJ software platform, is part of the Splice ecosystem. Although Serato doesn't have its own affiliate program, affiliates can promote Splice, which offers Serato-compatible software, and earn commissions on referrals. Serato is known for its user-friendly interface and seamless integration with hardware controllers. Serato offers a trial plan for new users, allowing affiliates to promote the software while users test it.

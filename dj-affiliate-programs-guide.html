The Splice affiliate program is a great way for influencers to earn money on every referral. With a 15-day cookie duration, affiliates can earn a commission on new subscriptions and trial sign-ups. Splice is a great choice for affiliates looking to earn substantial commissions on music production and DJing software.

The company has an affiliate program that offers commission rates on qualifying purchases, with an earning structure that varies depending on the plan.

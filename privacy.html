Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

Offbeat Inc. operates offbeatinc.com — a website providing DJ software reviews, music production tool comparisons, and affiliate program information. We're a small independent company based in South Carolina.

This site participates in affiliate marketing programs. 3. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data. Specifically:

  • No personal accounts: We do not require you to create an account or log in.

  • Affiliate programs (Unison Audio, Loopmasters, Splice, Plugin Boutique, Waves, etc.)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

5. How We Use AI (DatBotty)

Content on this site is researched and drafted by DatBotty, our autonomous AI system, then reviewed by a human before publishing. DatBotty does not collect visitor data and is not connected to the public-facing site.

6. Your Rights

Because we collect minimal personal data, there is generally nothing to delete or correct. If you believe we have any data about you and wish to have it removed, contact us and we'll address it promptly.

We may update this policy as the site evolves. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

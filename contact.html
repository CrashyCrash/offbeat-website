Corrections & Fact Checks

We take accuracy seriously. If you spot an error in our testing data, product specs, or pricing info, please send us the details below so we can verify and update quickly.

What to include in a correction request

  • The exact page URL and section heading
  • What's currently listed and what you believe is correct
  • A link to an official source (manufacturer spec sheet, press release, firmware changelog, etc.)
  • Your name or handle (optional — we credit fact-checkers)

Preferred sources: Manufacturer product pages, official PDF manuals, firmware release notes, or direct confirmation from brand reps. Third-party retailer listings (Amazon, Sweetwater, etc.) are helpful but we prioritize primary sources.

Accessibility Feedback

We want Offbeat Inc. to be usable by everyone. If you encounter barriers — screen reader issues, color contrast problems, keyboard navigation failures, or anything else — please let us know.

Details that help us fix issues faster

  • The page URL where the issue occurs
  • Your device, OS, and browser (e.g., "iPhone 15, iOS 18, Safari")
  • Assistive technology used (VoiceOver, NVDA, JAWS, switch control, etc.)
  • A description of what happened vs. what you expected

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to contact requests?

We aim to reply within 1–3 business days. Correction requests with verified sources are usually addressed within 24 hours. Partnership and press inquiries may take up to 5 business days during busy review seasons.

Do you accept free products for review?

Yes, but only as loaner units with no editorial conditions. We return or purchase review units after testing. Any gifted or retained product is disclosed on the relevant page per FTC guidelines and our affiliate disclosure.

Can I write a guest post for Offbeat?

We do not accept unsolicited guest posts. All content is written in-house by our team of working DJs and producers. If you have an exceptional educational topic, pitch it via email — but expect a 95% rejection rate.

How do you test DJ gear?

Every product is tested in real-world conditions: home studios, live gigs, and club environments. We run each unit for a minimum of 30 days before publishing a review. Read our full methodology on the About page.

Do you have a physical mailing address?

For review units and brand correspondence, email us first and we'll provide a shipping address. We don't publish it publicly to avoid spam.

I found an error on one of your pages. Will you credit me?

Absolutely. We maintain a fact-checker credit list and will add your name or handle (your choice) when we publish a correction based on your tip. Just mention it in your email.

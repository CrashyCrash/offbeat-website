Editorial Policy

Editorial Standards

How Offbeat Inc. researches, checks, updates, and monetizes its DJ gear, software, and workflow guides.

✍️ By Offbeat Inc. Editorial Team📅 Updated June 2026⏱️ 1 min read

Recommendation Standard

Offbeat Inc. recommendations should be based on practical fit, official product information, software compatibility, price context, upgrade path, and real-world DJ workflow constraints. A product can be excluded or criticized even when it has affiliate potential.

Testing vs. Evaluation Language

Pages should only claim direct testing when the page explains what was tested. If a page is based on official specs, documentation, compatibility checks, pricing, and editorial comparison, it should use language such as “evaluated,” “compared,” or “reviewed” instead of implying lab testing.

Source Links

Important review and comparison pages should include manufacturer, software, or platform source links where appropriate. Retailer links are useful for buying, but they do not replace official specs, compatibility notes, or support documentation.

Affiliate Policy

Affiliate links may earn commissions at no extra cost to readers. Those links should be labeled through the site disclosure system, use appropriate sponsored/nofollow attributes, and avoid hiding commercial intent.

Update Policy

Pages marked “Updated June 2026” should be rechecked before production launch and periodically after launch, especially when software plans, hardware compatibility, pricing, or availability changes.

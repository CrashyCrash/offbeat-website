How much does Splice's affiliate program pay?

Splice pays affiliates 20-25% commission on new subscription signups. Since Splice Basic starts at $7.99/month, a commission is roughly $1.60-$2 per referral per month. The real value compounds: if referred users stay subscribed for 12+ months, a single referral generates $19-$24 in total commissions.

How do I apply to the Splice affiliate program?

Splice manages affiliates through Impact.com. Create an account, search for 'Splice', and apply directly. Approval typically requires a music-focused website, YouTube channel, or social media presence with engaged followers. Most applicants with active content get approved within 3-5 business days.

What kind of content promotes Splice effectively?

Frequently Asked Questions

How are affiliate links labeled on Offbeat pages?

We use visible wording such as (affiliate link) or a disclosure notice near the section that contains retailer links. In the HTML, those links also use rel="nofollow sponsored".

Do prices change if I use an affiliate link?

No. The price is typically the same whether you use our link or go directly to the merchant. The retailer pays the commission, not the reader.

Can brands pay for a positive review or a higher ranking?

No. We do not sell positive coverage, guaranteed rankings, or hidden placements. If we are compensated for anything beyond standard affiliate commissions, that relationship should be separately disclosed.

What should I do if you missed a disclosure or used a broken link?

Email us at hello@offbeatinc.com or use our contact page. We would rather fix a disclosure problem immediately than leave it live.

How often do you review this disclosure page?

We review it whenever affiliate partners change and during broader content audits. The effective date and last-updated line at the top of the page are there so readers can see when the disclosure was last checked.

Full Review

zZounds is one of the largest online music gear retailers in the US with over 750,000 products. Their affiliate program consistently outperforms Amazon Associates for music gear publishers because of the 6% vs 3% rate and 45-day vs 24-hour cookie. Here's the detailed breakdown.

Commission Structure

zZounds pays 6% on most products including DJ controllers, mixers, headphones, studio monitors, and production software. DJ gear at average order values of $200–$600 generates $12–$36 per sale — 2–4× higher than Amazon's equivalent.

The Payment Plan Advantage

zZounds is famous for their Play as you Pay installment plans — customers can buy a $599 controller for $49/month with no credit check. This dramatically increases conversion rates on expensive items. You earn the full 6% commission on the total purchase price upfront (when the customer's first payment clears).

How to Apply

Apply through ShareASale (publisher ID required). Approval typically takes 2–5 business days. zZounds reviews each publisher manually — music gear, DJ, and recording content sites are approved at high rates. General coupon and cashback sites are often denied.

Best Practices for zZounds Links

Create deep links to specific product pages rather than the homepage. Category pages (like 'DJ Controllers') convert better than homepage links. List the payment plan prominently in your copy — customers respond strongly to 'Pay as low as $XX/month.'

Frequently Asked Questions

Is zZounds better than Amazon Associates for DJ gear?

Yes, for established DJ/music publishers. The 6% rate + 45-day cookie doubles or triples earnings on high-ticket items vs Amazon. Amazon still wins for convenience and brand trust with casual buyers.

Can I use both zZounds and Amazon affiliate links on the same page?

Yes. Many publishers run both: zZounds link for the primary buy button, Amazon link as a secondary option. This improves total conversion by giving buyers a choice.

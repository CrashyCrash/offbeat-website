What does zZounds affiliate program pay?

How do I join the zZounds affiliate program?

zZounds runs its affiliate program through CJ Affiliate (Commission Junction). Apply directly through the CJ publisher portal. A music-focused website with DJ or recording gear content is the ideal profile. Applications are usually reviewed within 5 business days, with high approval rates for relevant content creators.

What makes zZounds different from other music gear stores?

zZounds is unique in offering 0% APR monthly payment plans on most gear without a credit check. This dramatically increases conversions on higher-ticket items ($300+) because buyers who can't afford full price upfront can still purchase. As an affiliate, you earn full commission on the total sale price.

Does zZounds work well for DJ gear affiliate content?

How does zZounds compare to Sweetwater for affiliates?
